About me

Hello!

My name is Renata and this is my final project for CS50x.

Since I've always been a fan of silly simple games, I decided to check how I could make a game that could run in HTML. Then, I discovered Phaser. It handles most of the aspects of the game (like gravity!) so I could focus on the game itself, which I developed in JavaScript.

It was challenging, and I learned a lot in the process.

And I hope you have fun playing it.
